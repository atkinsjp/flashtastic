How does the AI study buddy work?

Our AI study buddy provides personalized tutoring through chat, offering explanations, hints, and encouragement tailored to each student's learning style and grade level. All AI interactions include content reporting features for safety.

Can multiple children use one account?

Yes! FlashTastic supports family accounts with individual progress tracking for each child, sibling competitions, and collaborative learning goals that bring families together.

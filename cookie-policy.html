Cookie Policy

Effective Date: September 2, 2025

Quick Summary: We use cookies to make FlashTastic work better for you - remembering your progress, keeping you logged in, and improving your learning experience. You can control most cookies through your browser settings.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and allowing certain features to work properly.

How We Use Cookies

FlashTastic uses cookies for several important purposes:

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They cannot be disabled as they are essential for core functionality:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in securely
  • Session Management: Maintain your session while using the app
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and fraud
  • Basic Functionality: Enable core features like navigation and form submission

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Learning Progress: Save your study sessions and quiz results
  • Avatar Customization: Remember your 3D avatar settings
  • Subject Preferences: Keep track of your favorite subjects
  • Display Settings: Remember your preferred theme and layout
  • Family Settings: Store family member profiles and competition data

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use FlashTastic so we can improve it:

  • Usage Analytics: Track which features are most popular
  • Performance Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Learning Insights: Understand study patterns to improve content

Payment and Subscription Cookies

When you subscribe to FlashTastic Premium or Family plans:

  • Stripe Integration: Secure payment processing
  • Subscription Status: Track your active subscription
  • Billing Preferences: Remember your payment method preferences

Third-Party Cookies

FlashTastic may use services from trusted partners that set their own cookies:

Stripe (Payment Processing)

When you make payments, Stripe may set cookies to:

  • Process payments securely
  • Prevent fraud
  • Remember payment preferences

Google Services

We use Google services for:

  • Analytics to understand app usage
  • AI-powered educational content generation
  • Performance monitoring

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Prevents all cookies (may break website functionality)
  • Block Third-Party Cookies: Allows our cookies but blocks external ones
  • Delete Existing Cookies: Removes cookies already stored
  • Get Notifications: Ask before cookies are set

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

⚠️ Important Note

Disabling essential cookies will prevent FlashTastic from working properly. You may not be able to log in, save progress, or access premium features.

Mobile App Considerations

If you're using FlashTastic as a mobile app (Progressive Web App or through app stores):

  • Cookie settings are managed through the app's built-in browser
  • Some mobile platforms have additional privacy controls
  • Data is stored locally using secure app storage in addition to cookies
